Kelley Senior Send-Off

The Kelley Senior Send-Off ceremony is hosted by the Kelley School of Business and is separate from IU Commencement.

Action required (registration)

Applying for graduation is not the same as registering for Kelley Senior Send-Off. If you received an email reminder, it means you have already applied for graduation but have not registered for Senior Send-Off yet. To participate, please complete the Kelley Senior Send-Off registration form. If you do not register by the deadline, the office will assume you are not attending and your assigned time may be released.

What the registration form covers

The form includes: (1) requests for an alternate ceremony time, (2) accommodations for yourself and/or guests, and (3) the phonetic pronunciation of your name. Please complete registration even if you are already assigned to your preferred time. Confirmation emails are sent weekly, so you may not receive an immediate response.
