Senior Recognition Ceremony (Kelley)

If you applied on time for graduation, you will receive a formal invitation by mail for either the December or May Kelley Undergraduate Senior Recognition, with directions on how to RSVP.

Senior Recognition Info (RSVP + Details) Email Kelley (ksbrec@iu.edu)
  • IU Commencement Program: Name + Hometown + Distinction Honors

    Your name, hometown, and distinction honors (if applicable) will appear in the IU Commencement Program. Your hometown will be listed as it appears in One.IU.

    Update Hometown / Address (Student Central)
  • Diploma: Official Name Changes

    If you wish to change your official name, contact the Student Central office. More information is available at the link below.

    Diploma Information (Student Central)
  • Diploma Mailing: Verify Your Address

    Diplomas are mailed by the Office of the Registrar. Please verify your address using Student Central. The Registrar will mail your diploma approximately three months after your graduation date.
