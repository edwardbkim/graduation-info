Kelley Senior Send-Off

Kelley Senior Send-Off is hosted by the Kelley School of Business and is separate from IU Commencement. Event registration is also separate from applying to graduate.

  • Next step

    If you would like to participate in Kelley Senior Send-Off, complete the Kelley registration form. If you received the reminder email, you applied for graduation but have not registered for Senior Send-Off.

  • Important

    Please complete registration even if you are assigned to the time you prefer. If you do not sign up by the deadline, the office will assume you are not coming and you may lose your assigned time.

What the registration form includes
The form includes important information like: (1) requests for an alternate ceremony time, (2) accommodations for yourself and/or guests, and (3) the phonetic pronunciation of your name.
Confirmation timing
The office sends confirmation emails on a weekly basis, so you will not get an immediate response.

IU Commencement (University-wide)

IU Commencement is organized by Indiana University and is separate from Kelley’s ceremony. For official IU undergraduate commencement details and what to expect, use the link below.

IU Commencement: What to Expect (Spring Undergrad)

Commencement Program & Diploma

  • IU Commencement Program: Name + Hometown + Distinction Honors

    Your name, hometown, and distinction honors (if applicable) will appear in the IU Commencement Program. Your hometown will be listed as it appears in One.IU.

  • Diploma: Official Name Changes

    If you wish to change your official name, contact the Student Central office.

  • Diploma Mailing: Verify Your Address

    Diplomas are mailed by the Office of the Registrar. Please verify your address using Student Central. The Office of the Registrar will mail your diploma approximately three months after the date of graduation.

FAQ

“We applied for graduation. Are we done?”
Applying for graduation is separate from registering for Kelley Senior Send-Off. If you want to participate in the Kelley ceremony, you must complete the Kelley registration form (even if you prefer your assigned time).
“Why did we get an email reminder?”
The reminder is sent to students who applied for graduation but have not registered for either Kelley ceremony time. Completing the registration is the next step if you plan to attend.
“When will we get confirmation?”
Confirmation emails are sent weekly, so you will not receive an immediate response.
